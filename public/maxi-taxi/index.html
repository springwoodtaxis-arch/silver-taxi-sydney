Maxi Taxi FAQs

Questions before you book a Maxi Taxi

Find quick answers about passengers, luggage, airport transfers, baby seat requests, accessibility options and fixed-fare quotes before you book.

How many passengers can a Maxi Taxi take?

Maxi taxi bookings can suit larger groups, commonly up to 7 to 11 passengers depending on vehicle availability, luggage volume and the exact booking requirements. Add your passenger count and bag count when booking so the correct option can be confirmed.

Can I book a Maxi Taxi to Sydney Airport?

Yes. Silver Taxi Sydney Service supports maxi taxi airport transfers to Sydney Airport terminals T1, T2 and T3, as well as Western Sydney International Airport transfer requests through the existing booking flow.

Do you offer baby seats or booster seats?

Child seat and booster requests should be added at the time of booking. Availability can vary, so pre-booking is recommended for family airport transfers and early morning trips.

Can I request a wheelchair accessible Maxi Taxi?

Yes, you can request wheelchair accessible taxi support. Please include ramp, chair and passenger details so a suitable option can be checked and confirmed before dispatch.

Is the fare fixed before pickup?

Silver Taxi Sydney Service focuses on confirmed direct booking and fixed-fare quote pathways where possible. Submit your trip details online or call 1800 173 171 for a quote before pickup.
