About Our Service

Premium Upper North Shore Taxi Service

Silver Taxi Sydney Upper North Shore provides a premium, reliable transportation solution across Sydney’s leafy, prestigious North Shore corridor. Stretching from Chatswood and Lane Cove through the garden suburbs of Killara, Lindfield, Gordon, Pymble, and Turramurra up to Wahroonga and Hornsby, this is one of Sydney’s most established residential precincts.

The Upper North Shore is well connected via the Pacific Highway and North Shore rail line, yet taxi service remains essential for airport transfers, school drop-offs, medical appointments at Royal North Shore and North Shore Private hospitals, Chatswood Westfield shopping trips, Macquarie Park corporate commutes, and after-hours travel when trains stop running.

We service every Upper North Shore suburb including Chatswood, St Ives, Turramurra, North Turramurra, South Turramurra, Pymble, West Pymble, Gordon, Killara, East Killara, Lindfield, East Lindfield, Roseville, Roseville Chase, Wahroonga, Warrawee, Waitara, Hornsby, Lane Cove, Lane Cove West, Artarmon, Willoughby, and Castle Cove — with direct transfers to Sydney Airport, CBD, Parramatta, Macquarie Park, and all Greater Sydney destinations.
