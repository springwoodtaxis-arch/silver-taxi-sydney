About Silver Taxi Sydney Service

Home About
Est. 2008

Sydney's Premier Silver Service

Silver Taxi Sydney Service was founded in 2008 with a simple but powerful vision: to provide Sydney with a taxi service that truly puts the passenger first. Not just a ride — an experience.

Over the past 16 years, we have grown from a small fleet of three vehicles to one of Sydney's most recognised premium transport brands, serving thousands of satisfied passengers every month.

Our drivers are not just drivers — they are professional chauffeurs who take pride in their presentation, their knowledge of Sydney and their commitment to making every journey exceptional.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Reliability

We show up on time, every time. Our on-time rate is 98.7% — because we know your time is valuable.

Genuine Care

We genuinely care about every passenger. From the greeting to the farewell, your comfort is our priority.

No hidden fees. No surge pricing. No surprises. Our fares are calculated using official NSW taxi rates.
