Which Terminal Are You Flying From?

Sydney Airport has three passenger terminals. Always check your boarding pass or airline app before heading to the pick-up zone.

T1
Terminal 1 — International
All international departures and arrivals. Meet & greet service available at the arrivals hall.
Airlines
Qantas International Virgin Australia Intl Singapore Airlines Emirates Cathay Pacific All international carriers
Pick-up: Ground level, Arrivals Drive
T2
Terminal 2 — Domestic
Used by most non-Qantas domestic airlines. Some Qantas codeshare or regional services may also depart from T2 — always check your boarding pass.
Airlines
Virgin Australia Jetstar Rex Airlines Link Airways Regional & charter services
Pick-up: Ground level, Domestic Drive
T3
Terminal 3 — Domestic (Qantas)
Mainly used by Qantas domestic flights and QantasLink. Some Qantas flights may occasionally depart from T2 — always check your boarding pass or the Qantas app.
Airlines
Qantas Domestic QantasLink

Common Questions

Airport Transfer FAQs

How much does a Sydney Airport taxi cost?

From 3 November 2025, Sydney Airport to Sydney CBD is a fixed fare of $60 for a sedan (1–4 passengers) or $80 for a Maxi/SUV. For destinations outside the Sydney CBD fixed-fare zone, the official NSW metered rate applies with all tolls included. No surge pricing, no hidden fees.

Do you track my flight for airport pickups?

Yes. We monitor your flight in real time. If your flight is delayed, your driver automatically adjusts their arrival time — at no extra cost to you. You'll receive SMS updates throughout.

Which terminal does my driver meet me at?

Your driver meets you at the arrivals exit of your terminal. T1 International — ground floor arrivals hall. T2 Domestic — arrivals level, near exit doors. T3 Domestic (Qantas) — arrivals level. Your booking confirmation will include your driver's name, vehicle, and contact number.

Can I pre-book a Sydney Airport transfer?

Absolutely. You can book online up to 3 months in advance. We recommend booking at least 24 hours ahead for airport transfers. Instant confirmation is sent to your email and phone immediately after booking.

Do you service all Sydney Airport terminals?

Yes. We service all Sydney Airport terminals 24/7 — T1 International, T2 Domestic (Virgin Australia, Jetstar, Rex), and T3 Domestic (Qantas, QantasLink). Simply select your terminal when booking and your driver will be there.

Can I book a Maxi Taxi for a large group?

Yes. Our Maxi Taxis accommodate up to 11 passengers with generous luggage space — perfect for families, corporate groups, and sporting teams. The fixed fare for a Maxi/SUV to Sydney CBD is $80. Book online and select "Maxi Taxi" as your vehicle type.
