Pricing

Fixed Fare on Booking

01

Book Online

When you book online at silvertaxisydneyservice.com/book-online, we estimate your trip duration, time, and vehicle type. Your fixed fare includes booking fees, tolls, and traffic time — ensuring you arrive on time.

02

Call Centre

When you call our Call Centre at 1800 173 171, we take your booking and provide a fixed fare which includes booking fees, tolls, and traffic time to ensure you're charged properly and your cab arrives on time.

Important — Cancellation & Refund Policy

No Refund on Late Cancellations

If you cancel your booking less than 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time, you will not be eligible for a refund.

In all such cases, a minimum of 45% of the total fare must be paid as a cancellation fee. This fee is non-negotiable and will be charged to the payment method on file.

Summary: Cancellations made 2+ hours before pickup — no charge. Cancellations made less than 2 hours before pickup — 45% of fare is payable, no refund issued.
